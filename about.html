Welcome to Universal Sign Language Translator (USLT)!


In this website you can translate sign language to english (text and sound) using your device's webcam.


About Us

This website was made by two school students with the aim to help the people who can only communicate with sign language and those who can not understand sign language. We got the inspiration to make this website when we realized that we ourselves were unable to understand sign language properly. Then we decided to make this website. While making this website we kept the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) of Reduced Inequalities and Partnerships for the Goals in mind. Our aim is to reduce the inequalities amongst us and create a better world to live.

This website is the result of the hardwork of two school students. Kindly do not copy it.
