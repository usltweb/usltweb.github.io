Welcome to Universal Sign Language Translator (USLT)!


In this website you can translate sign language to english (text and sound) using your device's webcam.

Instructions:


1. The background should be clear.
2. Please show your hand clearly and close to the webcam and avoid showing your face or other objects.
3. Click on the start button before showing a new letter, repeat this process for every new letter shown.
